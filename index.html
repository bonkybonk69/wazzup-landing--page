The Reality

Cities are full of people.
So why does everyone feel by themselves?

Even in busy cities, building a real friend group is hard work, especially when you have just arrived, work from home, or you are still settling in. WazzUp! is our attempt to make that part easier.

Three Easy Steps

From signup to a new mate.
No awkward small talk needed.

We tried to take out anything that gets in the way of just meeting someone.

1
🗺️

Look Around

Sign up, pick a few interests, then open the live map to see people, events and spots nearby that line up with what you are into.

2
💬

Say Hi

Chat inside the app first so there is no pressure to swap numbers. Take it at your own speed and only meet up when you are ready.

🎉

Head to a local meet up, hang out, and earn rewards for actually turning up. That is how a quick chat turns into a real friend.
